About Us

We are the JEMMS! That stands for Mace Thompson, Jayshon Valentine, Mujahid Udofia, Elizabeth Williams, and Stomie Murphy — five students who teamed up this summer to make something really special just for you. We wrote the story, did the voices, built the animations, and even coded everything ourselves! Our goal was to make learning fun and exciting, and we hope you enjoy every part of what we’ve created. We had a blast putting it together, and now it’s your turn to sit back, watch, and enjoy.
